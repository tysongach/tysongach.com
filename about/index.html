Hiya—I'm an audio engineer and project engineer at Walters-Storyk Design Group—a worldwide architectural acoustics firm in Highland, New York. I'm a graduate of Full Sail University with degrees in Recording Arts and Music Business. I've worked for places such as The Daily Buzz, a nationally syndicated morning talk show, C&C Custom Drums, building drums for The Blue Man Group, alongside many other freelance jobs including engineering for Sam Rivers. In high school I unofficially formed Gach Drums which specializes in exotic wood veneered drumshells with clientele across the nation. I also take pleasure in independent music, cycling, percussion, exploring mother nature, vinyl records, coffee and great design in all of its forms.
